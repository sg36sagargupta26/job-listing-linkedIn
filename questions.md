# LinkedIn Job Assistant — Browser Extension Requirements

## ✅ What it does (agreed)
1. Runs as a **browser extension** while you browse LinkedIn jobs
2. Reads the **job description** on the page
3. Compares it against your **CV** → returns a match score / gap analysis
4. On your click, **saves the job** to a sheet (Google Sheets)

## ❓ Questions (one at a time)

| # | Question | Answer |
|---|----------|--------|
| 1 | Which browser? (Chrome, Firefox, both?) | Chrome |
| 2 | Where is your CV? (I saw newCV.pdf on your site — use that?) | Use website (sg36sagargupta26.github.io) |
| 3 | Match scoring: simple keyword match or AI/LLM (OpenAI, Claude, etc.)? | DeepSeek V3 AI matching |
| 4 | Sheet destination: Google Sheets? | Yes, Google Sheets |
| 5 | What job fields to save to sheet? | Title, Company, Location, URL, Posted date, Description, Match %, Missing skills, Date saved |
